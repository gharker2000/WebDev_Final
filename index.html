The Student with a Vision


Having grown up in a small rural town in Southwestern Oregon, I know the value of hard work in accomplishing one's goals. I was involved in sports and extracurricular organizations since the moment I could walk and talk. I spent my free time outdoors exploring one of Oregon's many beautiful waterfalls and rivers with my friends.




I plan on graduating BYU with a Bachelors degree in Experience Design and Management with a double minor in nonprofit organizations and global women's studies. I plan on changing the world for the better for women across all countries, languages, and walks of life. I may not be the most talented person in the world, but the talents I do have will be used to bless others.
